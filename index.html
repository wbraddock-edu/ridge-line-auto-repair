Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Most routine maintenance (oil change, tire rotation, brake inspection) takes 1–2 hours. Repairs vary depending on parts availability and complexity. When you drop off your vehicle, we'll give you a realistic time estimate. We'll call you if anything changes — we won't keep your car longer than necessary.
We don't currently offer loaner vehicles, but we do have a comfortable waiting area with free Wi-Fi and coffee. For longer repairs, we can arrange local shuttle service to nearby Narrows businesses or your home within a 10-mile radius. Just ask when you schedule your appointment.
All repairs at Ridge Line Auto Repair are backed by our 12-month / 12,000-mile parts and labor warranty — whichever comes first. If something we repaired fails within that window, bring it back and we'll make it right at no charge. This applies to all major repairs. Routine maintenance items (oil, filters) are not covered, but the workmanship always is.
We can give you a rough estimate over the phone for common services like oil changes, brake pads, or alternator replacement. However, for accurate pricing, we need to see the vehicle. Labor times vary by make and model, and we won't give you a number we can't stand behind. Stop in for a free inspection and written estimate — it's always no-obligation.
Yes — we service all domestic and import vehicles: Ford, Chevy, GMC, Dodge, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, Subaru, Nissan, Jeep, and more. The only vehicles we don't work on are heavy-duty commercial trucks (over 1-ton) and diesel vehicles requiring specialized injection system work. When in doubt, give us a call.
We use quality aftermarket parts from NAPA and other trusted suppliers that meet or exceed OEM specifications — this is how we keep prices fair without sacrificing quality. If you prefer OEM (original equipment manufacturer) parts for your vehicle, we're happy to source them. OEM parts are typically 20–40% more expensive, but we'll quote both options and let you decide.
We take walk-ins and can usually accommodate oil changes and quick inspections without an appointment. For anything more involved — brakes, AC, diagnostics — we recommend scheduling ahead so we can reserve a bay and get your parts ordered in advance. Appointments typically mean faster turnaround and less waiting for you.
We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express), debit cards, and checks. We also offer financing through Synchrony Financial for approved customers — apply in-store for 0% interest on qualifying repairs over $500. We do not currently accept Apple Pay or Google Pay, though we're working on it.
